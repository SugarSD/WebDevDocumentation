Base Elements

<html>

This element is used to tell the browser where the HTML document starts and where it ends.
Ex:

1 <!DOCTYPE html>
2 <html>
3     <head>
4         <title>A Website</title>
5     </head>
6     <body>
7         <h1>Hello, World!</h1>
8     </body>
9 </html>

<head>

This element is used to give the browser necessary information about the site such as its title, link to a stylesheet, etc.
Ex:

1 <!DOCTYPE html>
2 <html>
3     <head>
4         <title>My Website</title>
5         <link href="style.css" ref="stylesheet">
6     </head>
7     <body>
8        <h1>This website has a title and a stylesheet to make me
  look nice!</h1>
9    </body>
10 </html>

<body>

This element is used to tell the browser that any elements between its starting and closing tag are to be shown as the website.
Ex:

1 <!DOCTYPE html>
2 <html>
3     <head>
4         <title>Now You See Me</title>
5         <link href="style.css" ref="stylesheet">
6     </head>
7     <body>
8        <h1>I'm sitting inside the body tags so ill show up 
  on your screen!</h1>
9    </body>
10 </html>
